School Anthem

MARY IMMACULATE, our Patroness

Our Mother, Queen and Guide,

Mary Immaculate, our Patroness,

Friend, ever at our side.

Seat of True Wisdom, you’re our motto clear,

Virtues you practised, we will hold most dear,

Mary immaculate to say that we are yours.

MARY IMMACULATE, we’ve named our School

We love, we cherish thee.

Mary Immaculate, a Girl’s High School

we pledge our loyalty,

Forming its students in both heart and mind,

Moulding our nature to be gentle, kind,

Mary Immaculate a Girl’s High School

We’re proud to say that we are yours!
